Understanding Your DTI Ratio

Your DTI ratio shows what percentage of your monthly income goes toward debt payments. Lenders use this to assess your ability to take on new loans.

DTI Range Status What It Means
0% – 36% ✓ Healthy Good position for new loans. Most lenders prefer this range.
36% – 43% ⚠ Caution Some lenders may still approve, but options are limited.
Over 43% ✕ High Risk Most lenders will decline. Focus on paying down existing debt.
💡 Tip: Adjust your monthly debt payments or income above to see how your DTI changes in real time.
