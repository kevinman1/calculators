Which Strategy Should You Choose?

⛄ Snowball — Pay off the smallest balance first, then roll that freed-up payment to the next smallest. You get early wins, which builds motivation and momentum. Often recommended for people who need encouragement to stay on track.

🏔 Avalanche — Pay off the highest interest rate first. Mathematically optimal: you minimize total interest paid. Best for those who are disciplined and want to spend as little as possible.

Both strategies work by rolling freed payments to the next debt. The key is to keep your monthly budget fixed and stick to the plan. Any extra money beyond minimums accelerates the strategy.

💡 Tip: If the same debt appears first in both strategies, the results will be identical. The biggest savings usually occur when a high-interest debt has a large balance.
