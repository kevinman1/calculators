Strategy Guide
⛄ Snowball Pay off the smallest balance first. Builds momentum with quick wins. Best for motivation.
🏔 Avalanche Pay off the highest interest rate first. Mathematically minimises total interest. Best if disciplined.
🪜 Stairstepper Pay off whichever debt will be eliminated soonest with your available extra budget. Frees up cash flow the fastest — re-evaluated after each payoff. A balance between snowball and avalanche.
💰 High Payment Pay off the debt with the highest minimum payment first. Frees up the largest monthly cash amount when paid off — good if your goal is maximising future cash flow.
